My Works

I am now studying Human Computer Interaction in the University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI). I used to be a journalist in China. Here are the introductions of my projects and work experiences.

Eatogether
UX Research & Design

Eatogether is a social dining application I am designing to help food lovers in college eat together and find more friends.
I have conducted user interviews with 13 people and now enter the stage of prototype.

Allergy
User Research & Design

My CHI team is designing a technological intervention for people who have allergy, to improve their quality of life.
I have conducted a user survey for 165 people and now enter the stage of user interview and design proposal.

Data Office
Contexual Inquiry

This is a consulting project which aims to find problems and solutions for increasing repeat users for Data Office, an organization providing medical database service.
My team has completed 4 contextual interviews.

Summit IDG
Web Design

I am designing a website for Summit IDG with WordPress, whose mission is to support, aid and honor Wounded Warriors and First Responders. I learned accessible design systematically in this contest.

Works in Media
News & Social Media

In the past 2 years, I worked as a journalist in China to report features on social problems and political analysis. I also operated online news accounts on a social media platform. I made mini-documentaries, when I studeid in Japan and France.
